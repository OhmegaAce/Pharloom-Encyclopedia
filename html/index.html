Description of the Game

Hollow Knight: Silksong is the long-awaited sequel to the award-winning action-adventure Hollow Knight. Players take on the role of Hornet, princess-protector of Hallownest, as she finds herself captured and transported to a mysterious new kingdom.

In Silksong, you’ll ascend through vast, haunting landscapes—from mossy grottos and shimmering coral caves to gilded citadels—facing over 150 all-new enemies and deadly bosses. Hornet’s acrobatic combat and silk-based tools open up fast, fluid movement and a fresh style of play compared to the Knight of the original game.

The journey is not just about survival. You’ll uncover the secrets of this strange land, forge unexpected alliances, and unravel Hornet’s own tangled destiny. With hand-crafted art, atmospheric music, and challenging exploration, Hollow Knight: Silksong builds on everything fans loved about the first game while pushing into uncharted territory.
